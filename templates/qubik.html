QUBIK

Shelving Unit

Qubik is a shelving unit developed as a part of "Bookclub" project. My first idea was to design a shelving unit for kid's bedroom.


I wanted to make use of as much space in the room I could. I started from a simple form of shelves from the ground to the ceiling. I moved bottom part of shelves outward in a form of stairs, so even the top shelves would be easy to reach. In first two levels of shelves I designed a pull-out system of shelves that can be used as chairs.


Units can be used as a provisional division of the room as I showed in the pictures below. I also designed similar forms for living room or bedroom.
