BATHROOM REFURBISHMENT

For this project I choose to refurbish my home bathroom. It is pretty small and located in the center of the apartment with no windows. The equipment and tiles are still original, installed in late 80s.


In blueprints below I showed current state of the bathroom and an idea plan for the refurbishment:

Bathroom being small, I wanted to gain some space in order for it not to look too crowded. I always felt some space could be saved with installing a shower instead of a bathtub so I planed an inbuild shower and moved the toilet from the enterance. I also wanted to think of a solution to hide the washing machine and add some storage.


Frontal views of western and eastern walls:

I picked smaller tiles; 10x10cm for the floor and walls and mosaic 2x2cm tiles for the shower to optically give an impression of a bigger space.
