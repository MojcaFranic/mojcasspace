A-ROUND BAR

One of the smaller spaces of ground floor ot the shop was meant to be a bar. Because shopping and running errands in the center of the town is a day activity I decided to design a coffee shop with bakery.


In blueprints below I showed current state of the space and my suggestion for the day bar:

I wanted to give customers options of seating, with parts of the bar giving them some privacy as booths.

Since the coffee shop is located on the other side of the art shop, I decided to continue the colour scheme. It is still mainly white with some more positive bright colours in the furniture. I opted for organic forms drawing inspiration from bubbles. Round forms of chairs and booths designed specially for the day bar inspired the name.
