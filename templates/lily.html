LILY

Ambiental Light

Lily was a part of a redesign group project in second year of interior design studies. It was a connection between "materials and development of interior design" studio and wood treatment course.


We got unused parts of chairs that were not being produced anymore. The task was to come up with an idea to use them in an object with different purpose.


We picked bended wood parts and started to assemble them together. They reminded us of a flower petals so we decided to put them in a group to form a flower. First we had to brush the wood so the pieces fit together. We treated the wood with a high gloss coat of white water-based wood paint. After it dried we glued them together and for the first model we used christmas lights inside the "petals".

designed by: Mojca Franic
