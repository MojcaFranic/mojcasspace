MULTIPURPOSE NIGHTSTAND

The nightstand was developed as a part of a home for the elderly. I wanted to design an object that is suitable for smaller spaces, since rooms in that type of living arrangements tend do be small. I wanted to design something that could be useful in more ways and would make living a bit easier for the user.


The nightstand is lifted with an electric motor mounted between the rear sides. This feature allows older people who can not bend, to access the lower drawers. At the same time it can be used as a smaller desk/table with the use of a lifted side part. Because of its multifunctionality, the nightstand is especially suitable for smaller rooms. We can use it as a nightstand, a lower club table or a small desk that can serve as a writing or dining table.
