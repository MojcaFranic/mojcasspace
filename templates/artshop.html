ART SHOP

In the bigger part of Ljubljana's Maximarket ground floor; one of the oldest shops, I designed an art shop. Since the Maximarket is an older, renowned institution I didn't want to "spoil" its reputation and look with crowded storage-like image. I imagined a gallery style shop with a single products of a kind displayed on the counters. Customers could try them out and mark what they want to buy on a designated paper or device. They would later give their list to an assistant at the counter who would bring their order from a storage in the back.


I started out with a suggested plan for departments of the shop:

While designing the shop I drew inspiration from art materials itself. The counters were drawn as brush strokes in the blueprint and later transformed into functional pieces of furniture. Colour scheme is mainly white as a blank canvas with some discreet yellow, orange and red strokes giving the space some life.

In frontal views I introduced the idea of over-sized mannequins floating from ceiling, playing with art material:

In windows I also wanted to use the art materials and supplies. One of the mannequins gives an impression to throw paint onto the pedestrians. I used pencils and brushes to design hats and other accesories for the mannequins.
