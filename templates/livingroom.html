LIVING ROOM REFURBISHMENT

As a part of first year design studio we were learning about basics of interior design and hand drawing through refurbishment of our own home. For one of my projects I decided to renovate my living room. The attic apartment was originally ment to be a studio, so all of the rooms were added later on in the unused spaces of the building. The apartment is located in the center of Ljubljana.


Photos of open plan living room, kitchen and balcony in current state:

Following pictures display blueprints of two suggestions for redesigning a kitchen and rearranging the furniture in living room. I wanted to gain some working space in the kitchen with adding a kitchen isle and moving the dining table in southern corner of the space. My goal in the living room part was to modernize the furniture and minimize storage space.

I decided on the first suggestion. Cube shelves mounted on the wall represent storage. With different sizes and positions they implement some dynamics into the space. Sunny yellow and orange colours bring warmth to the room.
