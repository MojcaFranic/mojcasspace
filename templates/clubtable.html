WOODEN CLUB TABLE

While a smaller renovation of my own living room I designed a couple of wooden club tables to replace the old one. I wanted enough space for drinks and snacks as it is a custom in Slovenia to have visitors sit in a living room rather than dining room. In the bottom there is enough storage space for books and magazines. The bigger table is accompanied by a smaller one next to the sofa. That one is meant for a reading lamp and a book, to be on the reach of the hand.

designed by: Mojca Franic
