ŠPORT2

Sport Hotel

Šport2 is a hotel, located above Srednji vrh near forrest Martuljek. Up in the mountains, connected to the nature and far away from city life guests can count on complete relaxation and active holiday. Surroundings offer a number of activities including trekking, alpinism, mountain biking and swimming in an indoor olympic pool. In winter months guests can visit nearby ski resorts.

I designed the hotel based on the element of the Earth, one of five elements of Feng Shui. Typical for that element are strong, cuboid forms. I wanted to express the element by playing and combining different rectangles in the space. I thought that "earth" colours are also the best to use in an object located in the nature. I used different shades of beige, brown and terracotta with an aim for the hotel not to stand out too much and have a relaxed feel to it. I also used natural materials such as stone and wood, which is also one of the local goods.
