INSTO

Insto was a new company from Ljubljana specializing in construction and engineering consulting. The customer was clear on what they expect from the logo. They had an idea of it including a ruler or some kind of engineering equipment. Through consultations we came to an agreement of the logo with a house as well. It went from simple outline to a more complex, modern object upon the customer's wish.
