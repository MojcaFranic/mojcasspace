HOME FOR THE ELDERLY

In this idea project I designed a suggestion for a home for the elderly. I imagined units, that can be connected together as needed. One unit has 8 sides and reminds of a flower in blueprint. One unit has:

- central part with communal area including reception, kitchen, dining room, relaxation/socializing areas and toilets,

- staff area and nursing bathroom,

- utility,

- 12 rooms; 10 single and 2 twin.

2 or 4 rooms have a joint terrace. 2 rooms share a wardrobe/storage space on the hallway connecting them. In the center there is a big communal kitchen with dining room and enough sitting space where people can read books, watch TV, socialize with other inhabitants or families that visit.

Central part is covered with a glass roof, so there is always enough daylight. Equipment is made of wood, bleached oak. Details in green colour bring some freshness to the space.

Unit can be a single object, serving a small community or joint together with others. That way some of them (the central one for example) can serve as functional part, joining residential ones together. By providing services as gym for physiotherapy, doctors offices, hairdressers, small shop and coffee shop, inhabitants have all they need in reach. My idea how to use space in the hallways that connect units is to arrange a sort of a green house where inhabitants could grow their own herbs, vegetables and flowers.
